## About the Book

When your father is a champion, whose game are you really playing?

*The Field* follows eleven-year-old Nikko as a quiet coach takes him to an abandoned field on a hillside — no bleachers, no scoreboard, no one watching — and teaches him that the ball is a partner, the voice in his head is not the truth, and honoring the people who came before him has nothing to do with becoming them.

Ten illustrated chapters. Ten lessons from the pitch. A complete training journal. For every young player who has ever wondered whether the path under their feet was really their own.

## Deployment

This site is a static HTML/CSS/JS site with no build step. Deploy by:

- **GitHub Pages:** Settings → Pages → Deploy from branch → `main` / root
- **Netlify:** Drag the repo folder to [netlify.com/drop](https://app.netlify.com/drop)
- **Cloudflare Pages:** Connect this repo in the Cloudflare dashboard
- **Vercel:** `vercel deploy` or connect the repo

No dependencies. No build. Just static files.
